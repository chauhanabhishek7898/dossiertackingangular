FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Dossier APP?

Dossier is an APP based bike booking service which can be easily downloaded from the APP store. You have to just log in, choose your source & destination, and book. Get a verified driver and vehicle with an ETA of max.15-20 minutes.

 How do I book a bike online from Dossier?

Dossier is an APP that offers reasonable and trustworthy delivery facilities. Steps to rent a vehicle:

  • Sign in to the Dossier APP via your mobile number.
  • Setup your source and destination location.
  • Enter a few additional details and confirm your booking.
  • Get a verified driver and vehicle with an ETA of max.15-20 minutes
 Does Dossier provide intercity or interstate service?

Yes. The intercity & interstate service permits you to deliver goods anywhere within 60 km radius from the source location.
